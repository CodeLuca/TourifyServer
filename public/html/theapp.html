What is Tourify?

Tourify is a brand new app released by the YRS team at YRS SwiftKey in 2014. Tourify uses your location, the weather, and other things to instantly plan a day of your visit to a place and you can even customize your tour.

Who brought you Tourify?

Tourify was brought to you by the lovely folks at SwiftKey. Check them out at SwiftKey.
